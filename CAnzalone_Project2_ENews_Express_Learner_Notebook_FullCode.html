Project Business Statistics: E-news Express¶

Marks: 60

Define Problem Statement and Objectives¶

To remain competitive in the digital news space E-news Express has designed a new landing page to attract new subscribers after seeing a decline in engagement recently. We need to analyze the effectiveness of the new landing page for gaining new subscribers. This includes analyzing how much time is spent on the old landing page vs new landing page and the conversion rate of users. We also need to understand how language impacts the conversion rate and the time spent on the new landing page.

Step 1: Define the null and alternate hypotheses¶

𝐻0 : Users spend less time or the same time on the new landing page than the old landing page

𝐻𝑎 : Users spend more time on the new landing page than the old landing page

Step 2: Select Appropriate test¶

the sample means from 2 independent populations when the standard deviations are unknown we need Two independent sample t-test

Step 3: Decide the significance level¶

The problem statement gives us a α = 0.05

Step 6: Compare the p-value with $\alpha$¶

In [33]:

    print(f'As the p-value {p_value} is less than the level of significance, we reject the null hypothesis.')
else:
    print(f'As the p-value {p_value} is greater than the level of significance, we fail to reject the null hypothesis.')

As the p-value 0.000263224705619001 is less than the level of significance, we reject the null hypothesis.

Step 7: Draw inference¶

Since the pvalue is < 0.05, we reject the null hypothesis. Hence, we have enough statistical evidence to say that the time spent on the new landing page is greater than the time spent on the old landing page.

Conclusion and Business Recommendations¶

Based on this analysis, we can say that uses spend more time on the new landing page than the old landing page. We can also infer that users are more likely to convert if they use the new landing page. We do not have enough evidence to say users converting is independent of language preferred or that the average time spend on the new landing page is different based on language preferred. I would recommend proceeding with the new landing page but evaluating the french and spanish page as you gather more data for possible improvements to be made based on these preferred languages.
